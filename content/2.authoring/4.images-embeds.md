## Markdown

Display images or videos using standard Markdown syntax.

### Images

#code
```mdc

```

::code-preview
![Snow-capped mountains in a sea of clouds at sunset](/mountains.webp)

```mdc
![Snow-capped mountains in a sea of clouds at sunset](/mountains.webp)
```
::
