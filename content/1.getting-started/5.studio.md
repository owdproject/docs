[Nuxt Studio](https://content.nuxt.com/studio) can connect to a Nuxt Content project for visual editing. It is **optional**: you can keep editing **`content/`** files in your IDE only.

If you enable Studio on the **`owd-docs`** repo, follow the [official setup guide](https://content.nuxt.com/docs/studio/setup). Studio is **not** required to contribute to OWD documentation.
